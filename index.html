The Industry Challenge

Breaking the Creative Revision Bottleneck

Highly paid Senior Designers and Project Managers are spending 40% of their day acting as glorified proofreaders. We are automating objective checking to clear the brush for subjective human creativity.

The Exponential Math of Manual QC

1 Main Idea × 3 Sizes × 2 Lengths × 3 Revisions × 2 People
= 36 Reviews per Asset

With standard ad variations and global localizations, this easily hits hundreds of cumulative manual revisions per concept. Error blindness becomes statistically inevitable.

The Active QC Solution

Unlike current Artstash agents that passively tag metadata, the Gatekeeper AI operates as an active conversational reviewer, leaving timestamped comments just like Frame.io.

  • 1. The AI Engine (Objective): Sets asset status to "QC Failed" and leaves timestamped comments instantly, @-mentioning creators to fix technical breaches.
  • 2. The Human Eye (Subjective): Reviews only the files that pass technical specs, conversing with the AI by replying to comments about narrative pacing and emotional resonance.

Two Paths to Commercial Value

We are launching Gatekeeper as a premium Managed Service to dogfood our AI engine at scale, establishing trust and zero-defect delivery before rolling it out as a standalone Software-as-a-Service.
